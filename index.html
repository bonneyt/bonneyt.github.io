Welcome to Our Farm

Located in central Indiana, Broke Ass Farm is a small, family-owned farm that raises horses, goats, sheep, chickens, and rabbits.

Our Horses

Our horses are the heart of our farm. Each one has their own unique personality and story. We provide them with spacious pastures, proper nutrition, and plenty of love. Whether they're in a show arena, or just grazing the pasture, our horses are an integral part of our farm family.

MGS Calipso (Cally)

Cally is our spirited 17-year-old Arabian mare who brings energy and excitement to the farm. She loves spending time in the pasture with her friends and is a true competitor at heart. With her rider Kierra, they've achieved numerous championship titles, showcasing their incredible partnership and Cally's natural talent. Her fiery personality and competitive spirit make her a standout both in the show ring and on the farm.

Sparky

Sparky is our charismatic 9-year-old Shetland pony stud who brings endless entertainment to the farm. As our newest resident, along with his pasturemate Henry the donkey, he's already made quite an impression. Full of spunk and personality, Sparky can often be seen strutting his stuff when the mares are around, showing off his confident and playful nature. His small size doesn't stop him from having the biggest personality on the farm!

Henry the Donkey

Henry is our gentle 8-year-old donkey who came to the farm with his best friend Sparky. Despite his shy and quiet nature, Henry has a heart of gold and brings a calming presence to the farm. Nothing seems to ruffle his feathers - he takes everything in stride with his characteristic peaceful demeanor. His sweet personality makes him a favorite among visitors who appreciate his gentle soul.

Our Goats

Our playful goats keep the farm lively with their antics and curiosity. They're excellent at clearing brush and providing us with fresh milk. Each goat has its own distinct personality, and they love interacting with visitors and farm staff alike.

Our Sheep

Our gentle sheep are the woolly wonders of the farm. They graze peacefully in our pastures and provide us with beautiful wool. We take pride in ensuring they have plenty of space to roam and proper care throughout the year.

Our Chickens

Our flock of chickens not only provides us with fresh eggs but also helps control pests in our gardens. They have a spacious coop and plenty of room to scratch and peck. Watching their daily antics is always entertaining!

Our Rabbits

Our rabbits are the quiet, gentle members of our farm family. They have comfortable hutches and plenty of space to hop around. We enjoy watching their playful behavior and taking care of their specific needs.
